HISTORY

Spider-Man, sometimes translated as Spider-Man,1112 is a character created by Americans Stan Lee and Steve Ditko,1314 and introduced in the 15th comic book Amazing Fantasy, published by Marvel Comics in August 1962.15 This is a superhero who uses his superhuman abilities, reminiscent of a spider, to combat other supervillains who pursue sinister ends. Its creation dates back to the early 1960s in response to growing teen interest in comics and the success of The Fantastic 4.16 Following his first appearance in Amazing Fantasy,17 Marvel decided to produce a solo series entitled The Amazing Spider-Man, the initial issue of which was released in March 1963.18 Several other comics related to the character have since been distributed. as well as other products that have led to the establishment of a media franchise.While it has multiple alternate versions that have led to the development of a multiverse,19 Spider-Man's origins and main traits have remained largely unchanged over time. Usually his secret identity is Peter Parker, a young New York orphan who acquires superpowers after being bitten by a radioactive spider,15 and whose ideology as a hero is reflected primarily in the expression "with great power comes great responsibility.
